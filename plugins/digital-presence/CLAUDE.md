# Digital Presence

Manage and optimize your online presence across all major platforms: LinkedIn, Instagram, X (Twitter), Facebook, YouTube, GitHub, and personal website.

## Available Tools/Skills

- **Presence Analyzer** - Audit current presence across all platforms (activity, engagement, brand consistency)
- **Social Content Writer** - Generate platform-specific content (LinkedIn posts, tweets, Instagram captions)
- **Brand Voice Setup** - Define and apply consistent brand voice/tone across all channels
- **Enhancement Advisor** - Get recommendations for growing each platform (content gaps, SEO, engagement)
- **Content Calendar** - Plan and schedule content across platforms (monthly view, collaboration notes)
- **Repurpose** - Convert content between formats (LinkedIn post → tweet thread → Instagram carousel)

## Configuration

- **Access**: Private plugin (contact michal@msapps.mobi for access/setup)
- **Platform integrations**: OAuth connections to each platform as configured
- **Data**: Aggregates analytics from all connected platforms

## Common Workflows

1. **Audit Current Presence**
   - Run Presence Analyzer
   - Review brand consistency across platforms
   - Identify underutilized channels

2. **Plan Multi-Platform Campaign**
   - Create Content Calendar entry
   - Use Social Content Writer to generate content per platform
   - Apply Brand Voice via Enhancement Advisor
   - Schedule across all platforms

3. **Maximize Content ROI**
   - Create one "hero" piece (long-form LinkedIn article or blog post)
   - Use Repurpose to create 5-7 format variations
   - Schedule across LinkedIn, X, Instagram, Facebook
   - Track engagement per platform

## Best Practices

- **Maintain brand consistency** - Use Brand Voice Setup to ensure messaging aligns across platforms
- **Schedule strategically** - Post at peak hours per platform (different for LinkedIn vs. Instagram vs. X)
- **Repurpose ruthlessly** - One piece of content can reach 5+ different audiences in different formats
- **Engage authentically** - Respond to comments on each platform; don't just broadcast
- **Monitor platform-specific trends** - Each platform has different popular content types
- **Drive traffic strategically** - Use each platform's strengths (LinkedIn for authority, Instagram for brand, X for trends)
