description: Generate a weekly content plan across all platforms

Create a 7-day content plan that distributes the user's content strategically across platforms.

## Input
Optional: $ARGUMENTS (a theme, specific week, or focus area)

## Process

1. **Read brand voice** — Check for `brand-voice.md` to understand topics, audience, and platform priorities
2. **If no brand voice exists**, ask the user:
   - What platforms do you want to cover?
   - What are your 3-5 core topics?
   - How many posts per week feels sustainable?

3. **Build the calendar** with these principles:
   - Don't post on every platform every day — that's not sustainable
   - LinkedIn: 3-4x/week
   - X/Twitter: daily or 4-5x/week
   - Instagram: 2-3x/week
   - Facebook: 2-3x/week
   - YouTube: 1x/week (if applicable)
   - Mix content types (insight, story, question, resource, behind-the-scenes)
   - Vary topics across the week

## Output Format

Create a table:

| Day | Platform | Content Type | Topic/Angle | Notes |
|-----|----------|-------------|-------------|-------|
| Mon | LinkedIn | Insight post | [Topic] | [Hook idea] |
| Mon | X | Tweet | [Topic] | [One-liner] |
| Tue | Instagram | Carousel | [Topic] | [Slide ideas] |
| ... | ... | ... | ... | ... |

After the table, include:
- **Theme of the week:** What ties these posts together
- **One repurposable anchor:** Identify one piece that can be adapted across platforms
- **Quick wins:** 2-3 posts that can be written in under 5 minutes

Offer to write any of the posts from the calendar immediately.
