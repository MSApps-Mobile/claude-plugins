# Digital Presence Plugin

**Status: Private**

Manage your entire online presence from Claude Cowork — analyze activity, score platforms, and create optimized content across LinkedIn, Instagram, X, Facebook, YouTube, GitHub, and your website.

## What it does

- **Presence Analyzer** — Scan and score your activity across all platforms
- **Social Content Writer** — Create platform-optimized posts with your brand voice
- **Brand Voice Setup** — Configure your unique writing style so content sounds like you
- **Enhancement Advisor** — Get actionable recommendations to strengthen your online presence
- **Content Calendar** — Generate a weekly content plan across all platforms
- **Repurpose** — Turn one piece of content into posts for every platform
