# Digital Presence Plugin

Manage your entire online presence from one place — analyze what you have, score it, and create better content across social media, website, and GitHub.

## What It Does

This plugin does two things: it **analyzes your current digital activity** and it **creates platform-optimized content** to improve it. It's not a "one post fits all" tool — it understands that LinkedIn, Instagram, X, and your GitHub README each need different content, and it can scan your actual profiles to tell you what's working and what isn't.

## Components

### Skills

| Skill | What it does |
|-------|-------------|
| **Presence Analyzer** | Scan and score your current activity across all platforms. Profile quality, content quality, posting frequency, engagement — everything gets a score with specific findings. Works with browser access (live scan) or screenshots. |
| **Enhancement Advisor** | Turn analysis into action. Rewrites your bios, drafts improved posts, builds a 30-day improvement plan with actual deliverables — not just vague advice. |
| **Social Content** | Write posts for LinkedIn, Instagram, X/Twitter, Facebook, and YouTube. Platform-native, not generic. |
| **Brand Voice Setup** | Interactive interview to capture your unique writing voice. Run once, and all content reflects your style. |

### Commands

| Command | What it does |
|---------|-------------|
| `/presence-audit` | Full audit: scan activity, score every platform, and deliver an improvement plan with rewritten copy |
| `/content-calendar` | Generate a weekly content plan across all your platforms |
| `/repurpose` | Turn one piece of content into platform-native posts for every channel |

## Getting Started

1. **Install the plugin**
2. **Run an audit** — Say "audit my digital presence" or run `/presence-audit` to see where you stand
3. **Set up your voice** — Say "set up my brand voice" and answer a few questions
4. **Start creating** — Ask for a post, run `/content-calendar`, or try `/repurpose` with existing content
