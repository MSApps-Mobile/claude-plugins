# LinkedIn Analysis Guide

## Profile Review Checklist

### Headline
- Does it say more than just a job title?
- Good: "Building AI tools that help dev teams ship faster | CTO @ CloudBridge"
- Bad: "CTO at CloudBridge"
- Does it include keywords people search for?

### About Section
- First 3 lines visible without "See more" — do they hook?
- Does it explain WHO you help and HOW?
- Is it written in first person (not third)?
- Does it have a clear CTA? (e.g., "DM me about X" or "Check out [link]")

### Featured Section
- Is it being used? (many people leave it empty)
- Does it showcase best work? (top posts, articles, links)
- Is it current?

### Experience
- Does it read like a resume or does it tell a story?
- Are achievements quantified?

### Banner Image
- Custom or default blue?
- Does it reinforce the professional brand?

## Content Analysis

### Post Performance Benchmarks (by follower count)
| Followers | Good Impression Rate | Strong Engagement |
|-----------|---------------------|-------------------|
| < 1K | 200+ impressions | 10+ reactions |
| 1K-5K | 500+ impressions | 25+ reactions |
| 5K-15K | 2K+ impressions | 50+ reactions |
| 15K+ | 5K+ impressions | 100+ reactions |

### Content Mix Analysis
Look at last 10-20 posts and categorize:
- Insight/opinion posts
- Personal story posts
- How-to/educational posts
- Promotional posts
- Engagement bait ("Agree?")
- Shared articles with commentary

**Healthy mix:** 40% insight, 25% educational, 20% story, 10% promotional, 5% engagement
**Red flag:** More than 30% promotional or more than 20% engagement bait

### Posting Patterns
- Best performing day of week?
- Best performing time of day?
- Are they posting during their audience's active hours?
- Any viral or breakout posts? What made them different?

## Engagement Quality
- Are comments substantive or just "Great post!"?
- Does the author reply to comments? (LinkedIn rewards this)
- Are comments from their target audience or random connections?
- Comment-to-like ratio above 5% = very engaged audience

## Red Flags
- No activity in 30+ days
- All posts are shared articles with no original commentary
- Headline is just "[Role] at [Company]"
- No featured section
- Default banner image
- About section is empty or just a resume copy
- Posting only promotional content
