# Website / Blog Analysis Guide

## First Impression (5-second test)
Within 5 seconds of landing, can a visitor answer:
1. What does this person/company do?
2. Who is it for?
3. What should I do next? (CTA)

If not → the homepage needs work.

## Homepage Review

### Above the Fold
- **Headline:** Clear value proposition? (not clever, not vague)
- **Subheadline:** Supports the headline with specifics
- **CTA:** Visible, clear action (not just "Learn more")
- **Visual:** Professional? Relevant? Fast-loading?

### Below the Fold
- Social proof (testimonials, logos, numbers)?
- Services/products clearly presented?
- About section or team?
- Secondary CTAs?

## Blog / Content Analysis

### Content Quality
- **Expertise depth:** Does the content demonstrate real knowledge?
- **Originality:** Unique insights or rehashed generic advice?
- **Usefulness:** Would a reader bookmark this or share it?
- **Length:** Appropriate for the topic (not thin 300-word posts, not padded 5000-word monsters)
- **Formatting:** Scannable? Headers, short paragraphs, visuals?

### SEO Basics
- **Title tags:** Descriptive, under 60 chars, include keywords?
- **Meta descriptions:** Present and compelling?
- **Headers:** Proper H1 → H2 → H3 hierarchy?
- **Internal linking:** Posts link to each other?
- **Image alt text:** Present and descriptive?
- **URL structure:** Clean slugs (/blog/topic-name not /blog/p=12345)?
- **Mobile responsive:** Looks good on phone?
- **Page speed:** Loads in under 3 seconds?

### Publishing Cadence
- How often are new posts published?
- Is there a consistent schedule?
- When was the last post published?

**Benchmarks:**
| Cadence | Rating |
|---------|--------|
| 4+/month | Excellent |
| 2-3/month | Good |
| 1/month | Minimum |
| Less than 1/month | Needs improvement |
| 3+ months gap | Inactive |

## Technical Health (if visible)
- HTTPS enabled?
- No broken links on main pages?
- Contact forms working?
- Social links present and working?

## Conversion Analysis
- Is there a clear path from visitor → lead/follower/customer?
- Email capture? Newsletter? Free resource?
- Contact page easy to find?
- Pricing (if applicable) clear?

## Scoring
| Aspect | Weight | What to Check |
|--------|--------|---------------|
| First impression | 25% | 5-second test, above the fold |
| Content quality | 30% | Blog depth, originality, usefulness |
| SEO basics | 20% | Title tags, structure, speed |
| Conversion path | 15% | CTAs, email capture, contact |
| Publishing cadence | 10% | Frequency, recency |
