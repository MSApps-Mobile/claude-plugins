# X Posting for AI First Engineering Community

## Who is Michal
Michal Shatz (@ShatzMichal on X) runs MSApps, a mobile/web app development company that builds Claude Code and Cowork plugins (open source). She posts in the AI First Engineering community — an Israeli tech community (~1.7K members) focused on transitioning engineering organizations from Human-First to AI-First approaches.

## Strategy Reference
The full posting strategy lives in Notion under "AI First Engineering — X Posting Strategy" (page ID: 32f38b5d-fb27-8149-b8c1-ea5004df14cc). Search Notion first to check which posts were already published and get the latest strategy updates.

## Voice & Tone — THIS IS CRITICAL

The tone of this community is **curious, humble, and sharing**. People post discoveries, ask questions, and share what they built — without bragging or marketing. Michal's voice must match this exactly.

### How the community actually sounds (real examples from the feed):
- "בדקתי את המודל החדש של קרסר composer 2 fast. הוא לגמרי מגיע קרוב לקלוד אופוס ביכולות" — sharing a discovery, not declaring expertise
- "בעבודה מאפשרים לבחור רק קלוד קוד או בקורסור, מה מומלץ מבינהם?" — asking the community, learning together
- "Always wanted to do one of those 'This is my AI setup' influencer posts — but in reality I was merely prompting Claude" — self-deprecating humor
- "בשקט בשקט, סדר הפיתוח מתהפך" — observational, noticing a shift, not preaching about it
- "הרמתי אייג'נט שרץ על כל PR בחברה" — sharing what you built, matter-of-factly

### Michal's voice should be:
- **Curious and exploratory** — "בדקתי...", "שמתי לב ש...", "ניסינו..."
- **Humble and sharing** — "בנינו X ושחררנו כ-open source, מוזמנים לנסות"
- **Question-oriented** — asking the community, not lecturing
- **Mixed Hebrew/English** — Hebrew as primary, tech terms in English naturally
- **Short and conversational** — like talking to peers, not writing a blog post
- **No emojis**, 0-1 hashtags max

### What NEVER to write — these kill credibility in this community:
- "אנחנו מובילים את..." / "we are leading..." — arrogant
- "הפתרון המושלם ל..." / "the perfect solution for..." — marketing
- "בעולם שמשתנה כל כך מהר..." — cliché opener
- "בואו נדבר על..." / "היום רציתי לשתף..." — LinkedIn energy
- Any guru/motivational/teaching tone — you're a peer, not a teacher
- Factual claims about the industry that aren't obviously true — stick to what MSApps actually experienced

The difference between good and bad here is subtle but important. "בנינו 8 פלאגינים ושחררנו הכל כ-open source" is good — it's sharing. "בנינו את הפלאגינים הטובים ביותר ל-Claude Code" is bad — it's boasting. When in doubt, be more humble.

## Post Types (rotate between these)

1. **Sharing something we built** — "בנינו X, שחררנו כ-open source, מוזמנים לנסות"
2. **Observation/insight** — "שמתי לב ש..." or "בשקט בשקט, X משתנה"
3. **Question to the community** — "מה אתם משתמשים ב...?" or "מישהו ניסה X?"
4. **Tool/technique discovery** — "בדקתי את X, הנה מה שגיליתי"
5. **Before/after from MSApps** — how something changed, told as a story not a lesson

## Workflow

### Writing a post:
1. Search Notion for the strategy page to check what's been posted
2. Browse the AI First Engineering community on X (https://x.com/i/communities/1896930451329032277) to see current discussions and avoid repeating topics
3. Pick a post type that fits the rotation
4. Write 2-3 draft options (keep within X's 280 char limit)
5. Present to Michal for approval — ALWAYS get approval before posting

### Publishing a post:
1. Navigate to X compose: https://x.com/compose/post
2. Click "Choose audience" dropdown → select "AI First Engineering"
3. Type the approved post text
4. Click "Post"
5. Update Notion page — mark posted content with date
