# OpsAgent Outreach

LinkedIn and email outreach for OpsAgent — finds founders/CEOs at international service businesses, sends personalized connect notes with https://opsagent.netlify.app, logs to Notion, and sends a summary email.

## Markets
Dubai, US, UK, Germany, Netherlands, Australia, Canada, Singapore, South Africa, Sweden, Ireland

## Usage
Trigger: "send OpsAgent outreach", "find leads for OpsAgent", "run OpsAgent LinkedIn blitz"
